News

Kiyaga's Wedding Preparations launched...

The wedding preparations of Kiyaga were launched on Sunday, 26th of March, 2017 at Kololo gardens.Kiyaga is set to wed his longtime fiancee Judith on Saturday, the 13th of May, 2017. Abazibu will definitely be part of the preparations.

Glitz and Glamour at Abazibu dinner...

It was all glitz and glamour at the annual Abazibu dinner, 2017 edition that took place on Saturday, 25th of March, 2017 at Hotel Triangle. It was a nice evening full of fun and interspersed with nice and thought-provoking talks from the guest speakers. The new leaders of Abazibu were also inaugurated. If you missed it, don't miss it next year.

New Abazibu Committee inaugurated...

Abazibu now have a new executive committee composed of; Kwesigabo Andrew as chairman, Lubega Julius as vice chairman, Kasozi Ivan as treasurer, Nyesigye Anthony as Secretary and Bahwerwa Felix as publicity secretary. The committee was inaugurated on 25th of March, 2017 and they will serve a one year non-renewable term. The handover ceremony was held at Hotel Triangle.

Abazibu elect new executive committee...

Abazibu elected a new executive committee to lead them for the next one year;2017-2018. The elections were held on Saturday, the 11th of February, 2017. They were free and fair and these gallant gentlemen emerged victorious; Kwesigabo Andrew as chairman, Lubega Julius as vice chairman, Kasozi Ivan as treasurer, Nyesigye Anthony as Secretary and Bahwerwa Felix as publicity secretary.

Blakie's wedding...

Blakie wedded his lovely fiancee Esther Asiimwe on Saturday, the 28th of January 2017 at Uganda Martyrs Bassilica and thereafter hosted their guests to a sumptuous reception at Gloria gardens. Abazibu represented fully ofcourse. Best marriage wishes for Blakie.

Over Shs.1m raised in the "#SaveSseruwagi" Campaign...

The fundraising drive to raise funds assist our very own; Sseruwagi who had a terrible accident in footing his hospital bills, and handling his other affairs has raised over shs.1m in cash and in kind. Abazibu really went out of their way to show what it means to be for each other in both the good and the bad. Abazibu Forever...

Yet another kwanjula...

Abigaba Jude aka Shaka's fiancee introduced him to her parents over the weekend in a very colourful ceremony that took place in Abayita Ababiri, along Entebbe road. Of course, we were available to accompany our brother

Kido appointed DPC Sheema District...

ASP Innocent Mubangizi has been appointed DPC Sheema District effective immediately in a recent reshuffle done in Uganda Police.

Launch of the Abazibu Social Fund...

On Saturday, the 14th of May, 2016, Abazibu launched a fund that they dubbed "Abazibu Social Fund." This fund's purpose is to assist the members of the social fund in organizing their functions; be it celebratory or funeral ceremonies.

Abazibu pledge 5 million shillings...

We always aim to lead and others follow, so was this time also. On 19th March, 2016, it was St. Joseph's day, a day that is celebrated in Jovoc. This time it was special because the main hall project was also launched, and abazibu pledged 5 million shillings towards the cause.
